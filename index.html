Here Are The Woman That Fight For Freedom According To History

NANNY QUEEN OF THE MARRONS QUEEN NANNY THE MOTHER OF ALL JAMAICAN, Nanny Eventually Founded a vilage in the Blue Mountains In the late 17th Century a Young Woman Her Four Brothes where bought by the British slave Traders and sold to Plantations Owners in Jamaica but as faith would have it the young woman is now celebrated as a heroine by Jamaicans in 1975. The Jamaican Goverment Decleard Queen Nanny a 17th century slave from the Asante Kingdom in the Gold Coast wich is Ghana Today. A nation heroine owing to the fait of her fight against White Suprimacy And Slavery in Jamaica. Nanny is the only woman recognized among the seven nation heroes of jamaica, such a thing as Ghana's so called Big Six. Nanny had been taken as a slave along with her Four brothers to jamaica They toiled on lands from whoes fruits they would not eat. Nanny Eventually fonuded a vilage in the Blue Mountains on the eastern side of jamaica, which became known as Nanny's Town by 1720 Nanny had become the leader of this maroon settlement where she Trauned her Marron warriors in the art of Guerlilla warfare due to incessant tension between her pepole and the british as the marroons grew in their numbers the british colonial administration became threatend and upon further calls by plantation owners who were losing slaves and corps british foreces attacked jungulesnin jamaica ventually Nanny Was Killed In One Of The Conflicts With The British Her Brother Cudjoe, Was then forced to sign a peace treaty with the british which Nanny;s pepole being relocated to a place that would be called New Nanny Town Today. In Jamaica Nanny Is The Only Woman Whose Image Graces A Currency In The $500 Jamaica Note specifically in jamaica the Pepole Of Jamaica Are Greatful For an Asante Woman Their Ancestor Who Was Forced To Come Over Land Seas and contribute to ther emancipation.
